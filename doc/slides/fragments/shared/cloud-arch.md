# The cloud service

<span class="subtitle">Content delivery, owner page, and a viewer experience</span>

---

## Content delivery

![w:820](assets/diagrams/cloud-poll.svg)

- The **EZRGB cloud** pushes content; the **local player** polls for it
- The local player posts status to a **player server** in your region
- Phones and PCs talk to that server to manage your show over the internet

---

## The viewer page

![w:820](assets/diagrams/cloud-viewer.svg)

- (Optional) The player server also backs a public **viewer page**
- Show info, song voting, and audio — all from the visitor's phone
